NINE to FIVE

[View site]

NINE to FIVE is an attempt at creating a fully-functional website template. The project began in 2015. At this stage, development is on-going, with additions being made on an ad-hoc basis. The main goal was to further my development skills, whilst also creating a product that will eventually become marketable. The website itself was built using the front-end framework, Bootstrap, and features components such as responsiveness, animations, and a self-validating contact form.

Website templates are quite a common occurance in the web development world, with marketplaces being dedicated to the buying and selling of these products. Having always been fascinated with these marketplaces, I decided to try my hand at creating a template of my own. As it turns out, there is quite a lot of work involved – including, but not limited to, the design, development, and on-going maintenance of these products.

Just like most products today, there are items to suit every need. The same can be said for website templates, which cover categories from portfolios to fully-developed websites. NINE to FIVE was developed as a small business website, and currently only offers a single page platform. Eventually, the website will be scaled to involve multiple pages, which will offer greater diversity and flexibility in its use.

The current iteration of the website sees content divided into several sections, including a home page, an ‘about’ section, a ‘product’ section, a ‘contact’ section, and several rows of links at the bottom. As the website is navigated, content appears dynamically, which allows for an interesting and fun way to display information.

The best aspect about website templates is that they can be used for a wide variety of reasons, providing developers with a lot of flexibility. At this stage, NINE to FIVE will continue to be developed as a small business website, but may be used for other purposes in the future.
