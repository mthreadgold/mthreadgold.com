JAPANESE TRAVEL BLOG

[View site]

This blog was created in 2014, to highlight the memories of a school trip to Japan. The trip lasted a total of 10 days, with photographs and accompanying text being provided for each day. The website itself was developed using the WordPress platform. Graphic design was also a large component of the project, which was performed using Adobe Photoshop.

The design and development of this project was streamlined, thanks to WordPress and its wide variety of website templates. Once a suitable template was chosen, customizations to the overall style occurred â€“ these included a custom header image, custom icons for each blog post, and font styles.

The project was completed on a day-by-day basis. For each day, content was received, checked for quality, organized, and uploaded. Brief descriptions of each day were provided, which formed the basis of each blog post. Accompanying images were also provided. Together, these assets told the stories of the group involved, as they travelled to various locations in Japan.
