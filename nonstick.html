Nonstick.com is a website that is dedicated to the beloved Looney Tunes franchise. The site boasts a wide range of information and media content, allowing users to relive their childhood memories at any time. In 2015, an overhaul was performed on an existing static website, and transformed into a modern, responsive, and mobile-friendly version of its former self.

Initially, this project involved the migration of content from one server to another. At this time, no new content was necessary, and the migration was performed smoothly. The content being migrated included images, text, audio, and video â€“ all of which were spread throughout many pages.

Once the content was hosted on the new website, content optimization was performed. This process ensured that each element of content was being displayed correctly, as depicted by the previous version of the website. Components such as character pages, audio playlists, and tables were carefully designed and developed to match the existing styles.

nonstick.com

Soon after the new website had been developed, an online store was added to the website. The store allows users to purchase Looney Tunes related products through the use of Amazon Affiliate links.

After specifications for the store were laid out, the online store was developed, and graphics were designed, providing custom-made icons for each category of product. In total, there were 9 categories, with each category displaying anywhere from 15 to 70 products.

At this stage, nonstick.com is enjoying its overhaul, and continues to provide trips down memory lane to fans of the Looney Tunes franchise, both old and new.
